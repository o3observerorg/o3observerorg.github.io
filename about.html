Ozone Basics

Learn about the crisis

There are various kinds of air-related crises, ozone destruction is a thing that is a severe one as it can lead to a series of bad consequences.

According to the Encyclopedic Entry, the ozone layer is "one layer of the stratosphere, the second layer of the Earth’s atmosphere. The stratosphere is the mass of protective gases clinging to our planet”. Ozone layer mainly serves two functions. The first is that ozone layer is able to absorb ultraviolet light, in this way, it can protect all the the organisms on earth from the threat of being affected by ultraviolet light since over exposure to ultraviolet light is extremely harmful to living things.
